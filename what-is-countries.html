What is "Countries"?

Countries is a simulator game where players can create their own country and decide the future of it. By responding to new problems or approving ideas of the parlement, the player controls the fate of one’s country.

The players also have the ability to do much more things than just solving problems. Each country will have it’s own banking system which the player would have to manage carefully, every country would also have a fixed map of the borders, while displacing the military troops on the map. The player can move the troops around to stop potential revolts, or even invade a neighbouring country! Being boarded on your own? The game also provides a global network where players can find their friends, meet new friends and form their own organization with a unified goal to govern together, or not.
